Thank you for using Pano!
Pano is a research project from MIT CSAIL's Haystack Group for teaching people about moral framing while reading news and conversing.

Pano is a tool that allows you to choose to converse with others by leaving annotations on pages using the Pano tool while you are browsing the web.

Guarantees to you as a participant in this research project:
- Your participation is voluntary. You may uninstall Pano and abandon the application at any time.
- Please email eyebrowse@mit.edu with any concerns or questions.
